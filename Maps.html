The size of the dot represents the electricity generated for 500,000 people. By hovering over the dot, you can see the Electricity Per Person and the Total Electricity generated.

This graph compares Electricity Needed versus Electricity Generated. A blue dot represents the country generates more electricity than what is needed and the purple dot represents the country generates less electricity than what is needed.

This graph is a different way of representing the same information as in the previous graph. The map shows the color of the value of Electricity Need vs Generated. The darker shade of blue represents that the country generates more electricity.
