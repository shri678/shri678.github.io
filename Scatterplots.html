I'm curious to know about a country's well being. The 2 variables I chose to understand it were:

  • Human Development Index (HDI), a statistic composite index of life expectancy, education, and per capita income indicators. The top “ranked” 66 countries are considered to have a high HDI.
  • Employment rate. A country has full employment if that country has an unemployment rate of 5% or less.

This compares the HDI & Employment rate of all countries for the year 2007.
